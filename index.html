How to buy

There are a few easy ways to buy $KENDU. Whether you’re on Ethereum, Solana, Base, or just getting started through our website, you’ll find simple steps below to get what you need and join the Kendu movement.

Steps to buy Kendu on Ethereum

1

Get Phantom or your favourite wallet from the App Store or Google Play. For desktop users, visit phantom.com to download the extension.

2

Send Ethereum to your wallet to swap for $KENDU. You can buy Ethereum on an exchange such as Coinbase and send it to your wallet. Alternatively, you can purchase Eth directly in the widget's 'Buy' tab.

3

Connect your wallet using the 'Connect' button on the widget. Enter the amount you'd like to buy, and approve and sign all prompted transactions.

How to bridge

After buying Kendu, you can optionally bridge to other chains for trading. Long-term holders don’t need to bridge—there’s no benefit to bridging unless you're actively trading and want lower transaction fees on Base or Solana.

Steps to bridge Kendu on Ethereum

1

Connect your wallet with $KENDU token holdings to the Wormhole Connect widget.

2

Select your origin and destination chain and asset, for example, $KENDU on Ethereum to $KENDU on Base.

3

Enter your destination wallet address and approve all transactions. Please note that bridging can sometimes take 15-30 minutes or more, depending on chain activity.

ℹ
  • If you choose to bridge to Solana, you will need to enter a Solana wallet address to receive your bridged tokens.
  • Please do not nagivate away from the web page until your bridge transaction has fully completed.

Crypto is a volatile market, and memecoins even more so. You must be emotionally prepared for volatility to experience the enormous upside on the other side. Markets naturally fluctuate, similar to how humans breathe. After a sharp rise (a big breath in), people may take profits, leading to price corrections (breathing out); this process is called redistribution. It allows new members to enter at lower prices, redistributing tokens from large inactive early holders to new, active community members. Looking back on the charts of top successful projects, including Bitcoin, you'll notice multiple periods of redistribution and consolidation following parabolic moves to the upside. Each time a token/coin goes through this and comes out on the other side, it strengthens.

It's essential to zoom out and focus on the bigger picture during volatile periods. You're likely witnessing market consolidation, which is a healthy phase following significant price increases. During consolidation, a token finds its new price floor before rising to new heights in a "price discovery" phase. Trust in yourself and the community and continue contributing to the daily work. We can't control market forces or individual actions, but we can control our collective efforts and personal initiatives.
